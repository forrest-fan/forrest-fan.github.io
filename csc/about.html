At the Computer Science Centre, we aim to educate, entertain, and challenge our guests in the world of computer science through our hands-on experiences and engagement with coding. Our values and culture are reflected through our world-class thrills, fun, and family entertainment guided by the principles of uncompromising safety, outstanding customer service, cleanliness and integrity. Our model brings together attractions, entertainment and quality food under one roof, serviced by an operating team that understands the importance of the guest service experience.
