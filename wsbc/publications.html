About WSBC Publications

WSBC is proud to present intriguing articles about the sports industries written exclusively by WSBC members. Our publications offer a view into the world of sport business with in-depth analysis on current events and issues facing the industry as well as exclusive interviews with sport business leaders across a variety of roles and sports.


Our current lineup includes

  • Out of Bounds Newsletter - A monthly newsletter by Kenny Zhong that does a deep dive into issues surrounding the sport business world with insights from industry experts
  • Quick Hitters - Kieran Drover gives us a glance at the hottest headlines circulating sport business right now
